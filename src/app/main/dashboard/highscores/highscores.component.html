INSTRUCTIONS

  • The objective of this game is to shoot the incoming UFOs and take down as many as possible.
  • The game has 10 difficulty settings, each with its associated scores for each hit, speeds of the player and UFOs.
  • In the starting Level, UFOs come in straight and as level increases, the UFOs start to move around. The level increases as the score crosses each 100.
  • You have 3 lives. Your score increases as you hit any UFO. Your life gets reduced when you crash with an UFO. Also, you can only shoot 5 active bullets at a time.

  • Controls:

    Move left:   ←   or   a
    Move right:   →   or   d
    Move up:   ↑   or   a
    Move down:   ↓   or   s
    Shoot:   space   or   ↲
    Boost:   shift
  • Controls:

    • Click and drag your Space ship to move.
    • The Space ship will fire at 2 shots per second.
